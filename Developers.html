    Investing in the future of Somaliland

Developers - Become a Partner

Many Somalilanders living abroad are keen on buying a property of their own in Somaliland. However, many of them have been discouraged by the ugly stories they have heard about how people have lost money through the activities of some dishonest developers. Stories abound of how these developers have mismanaged buyers' funds or have delivered inferior quality homes. This has made people very cautious about dealing with local developers resulting in very slow sales.

However, it is a well-known fact that Somalilanders in diaspora remit over $500 million home annually. At Diaspora Property Developers, our goal is to help local developers overcome these obstacles and enjoy better/quicker sales especially abroad. To achieve this we provide a service that is designed to bring buyers worldwide and local developers together. The Major benefits of using our platform include

  • Quicker sales of units
  • Better access to Diaspora market worldwide
  • Increased pre-construction sales
  • Lower marketing costs
  • Greater customer satisfaction

    • Diaspora Property Developers is committed to making it safer and easier for Somalilanders living anywhere in the world to buy off-plan property from local developers. With our platform powered by our innovative model, you can easily tap into the Diaspora market to sell your properties quickly and efficiently.
