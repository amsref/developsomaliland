Why Choose Us?

Somaliland Diaspora Property Developers was established to provide exceptional Real Estate solutions to Somalilanders living abroad and foreigners who want to invest here. We identify prime land, commercial, residential and industrial properties to best suit our clients investment objectives

  • We have a team of dedicated professionals to assist you through the whole process and guide investors in the Diaspora through purchase options
  • We work in partnership with experienced architects, builders and legal experts to provide a personalised services for every purchase.
  • We carefully vet all transactions and purchases properties to ensure your funds are in good hands. We also help and manage the project from start to finish, you can be rest assured that there is little to worry about - we will provide you with regular updates throughout the process to keep your mind at ease.

Mission

Our mission is to help the Somaliland diaspora community reconnect with their motherland. Buying property in Somaliland can be very stressful and challenging and our mission is to make the whole process as easy as possible. We do all the legwork so you don’t have to and the property you want is only just a click away.

Safety

The process of acquiring property in Somaliland is fraught with all manner of risks and many stories abound of people who have lost their money to unscrupulous developers/agents. That’s why our model is designed to address many of the risks buyers currently face when buying off-plan property. With our unique approach, you can safely acquire your desired property without fear of loss.

Expertise
